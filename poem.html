A Poem

Just a single Poem!!!
Read and enjoy!

  • Episode 1

    I was born to be of service

    Depot Zaria felt like home

    I had Honour

    I found purpose

    Sir Yes Sir, That's all I know

    They sent us for peacekeeping

    In an unnamed African country



    We were taught to shoot our rifles

    Then in one, side by side

    I got this painful ringing in my ears

    From a grenade blast last night

    But the armoured vehicle

    Could not save my best friend's life



    It's 22months now, I got home

    With all these medals on my chest

    But there are things i can't remember,

    And there are things i can't forget

    I lay awake at night with dreamsEven devil couldn't see

    With these prayers

    I hope i can be unbroken



    My brothers-in-arms has done more for me

    Than the medication would

    Today 22(PTSD) would die from suicide

    Just like yesterday the were gone

    I live my live for each tomorrow

    So their memories would live on

    Once we were boys, and we were strangers

    Now we brothers and we are men

    One-day you would ask me was it worth it

    To be of service in the end

    Well the blessings and the curses

    I would do it all again
